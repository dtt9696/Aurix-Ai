# Corporate Risk Diagnosis Domains & Sources

## 1. Business Registration & Integrity (Business Registration)
- **Source**: OpenCorporates, SEC EDGAR.
- **Key Data**: Registration status, parent/subsidiary hierarchy, officer/director names.
- **Risk Indicator**: Unusual ownership structures, shell company signatures, frequent officer changes.

## 2. Judicial & Litigation (Litigation)
- **Source**: CourtListener (RECAP), Federal Court Dockets.
- **Key Data**: Civil lawsuits, criminal charges, bankruptcy filings (Chapter 11/7).
- **Risk Indicator**: High-value pending litigation, pattern of labor violations, environmental lawsuits.

## 3. Sanctions & Compliance (Sanctions/Compliance)
- **Source**: OFAC (Department of the Treasury), Federal Register.
- **Key Data**: SDN (Specially Designated Nationals) list, SSI (Sectoral Sanctions Identifications).
- **Risk Indicator**: Direct or indirect nexus with sanctioned entities or regions.

## 4. Financial & Market (Financial/Market)
- **Source**: SEC filings (10-K, 10-Q), FRED (Federal Reserve).
- **Key Data**: Altman Z-Score components (Liquidity, Solvency, Profitability), Interest rates.
- **Risk Indicator**: Z-Score < 1.8 (Distress), negative cash flows, high debt-to-equity ratios.

## 5. Supply Chain & Trade (Supply Chain)
- **Source**: UN Comtrade, 10-K Risk Factors.
- **Key Data**: Concentration of major suppliers, geographic dependencies.
- **Risk Indicator**: Reliance on single-source suppliers in volatile regions.
